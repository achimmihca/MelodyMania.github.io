Player Profiles

  • Configure player profiles to quickly switch between players during the game.
  • You can disable player profiles in the settings. Only enabled player profiles are available during song select.

Custom Player Profile Images

  • You can add custom player profile images (PNG or JPG files).
    • TIP: Browse player profile images and other user-generated content on Steam Workshop.
  • You can add a profile image using a webcam or integrated camera of your device.
    • Therefor, select the the very last profile image in the list and take a snapshot.
  • Player profile images are searched in specific folders:
    • <mod-folder>/PlayerProfileImages
    • <data-folder>/PlayerProfileImages
    • <installation-path>/Melody Mania_Data/StreamingAssets/PlayerProfileImages
  • To install a player profile image, copy it to one of these locations. A restart of the game may be required afterwards.
