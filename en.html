Enjoy a Fun Singing Party!

Want to 

Melody Mania is a singing game with boundless possibilities!

Players sing along their favorite songs and try to hit notes for points. Feel like a superstar while you break the highscore! Invite your family and friends to rock the virtual stage together!

Follow the project on

With Melody Mania, YOU'LL GET:

  • Pitch detection to guide your voice
  • Custom songs and song editor
  • Integration of websites (e.g. YouTube)
  • Companion App as mic
  • Cooperative score mode
  • Team and tournament game modes
  • Song queue and medleys
  • Favorites and playlists
  • AI powered isolation of vocals
  • Controller and webcam support

Imagine what it would be like to...

  • Throw a singing party with family and friends
  • Break the highscore or sing duets together
  • Create the songs you want, no matter your taste in music
  • Use your smartphone as mic, no need to buy new equipment
  • Mute the vocals of your local songs for a classic karaoke experience
  • Use video platforms such as YouTube directly in game

Solos, Duets, Groups

Sing a duet where each player has different lyrics and notes. Or throw a party and sing with up to 16 players at the same time.

Entertained Audience

Each song can have a background video or image. A delight for both, the audience and the singers.

Score Modes

A cooperative score mode emphasizes SINGING TOGETHER with your loved ones. Or enjoy classic karaoke without any scores.

Team & Tournament

Play multiple rounds, round robin style, or a knock-out tournament. Fun for groups of all sizes!

Spice Up the Game

Put your skills to the test with twists to the regular game, for example by hiding lyrics and notes for the leading player.

Song Queue & Medleys

Play multiple songs from the queue without interruption. Link songs in the queue for a medley.

Videos on the Internet

Use videos from YouTube or other websites. This is possible thanks to the browser that has been embedded in the game.

Companion App

Search and enqueue songs while others are still singing. Use your smartphone as mic or to control the game remotely.

Use Existing Microphones

Connect your smartphone as mic. Use headsets or karaoke microphones from other games.

Open Song Formats

Melody Mania supports the widespread and open UltraStar format, as well as the industry standard MIDI format.

Webcam

Singing is even more fun when you can see yourself! Use your camera as background or take a snapshot for your profile image

Vocals Isolation

Split your mp3 or ogg files into vocals and instrumental track. Useful to mute vocals for classic karaoke or when editing songs.

Playlists & Favorites

Mark songs as favorites and organize your song library with playlists in M3U or UltraStar format.

Player & Mic Profiles

Configure each player and device individually. Quickly reassign microphones to players.

Advanced Search

Handle large song collections with ease. Quickly jump to songs, or apply filters to find duets, 90s hits, or rock songs, for example.

Song Editor

The integrated editor lets you create a sing-along version of any song you like - unlimited possibilities!

Dictate Lyrics

Use speech recognition to dictate lyrics synchronized with the music.

Themes

Change the look and feel of the game to suit your style. Create custom themes.

Controller Support

No need to hang around your keyboard. Just use your gamepad to control Melody Mania.

Create your own songs

Create and edit songs as you please. The AI POWERED EDITOR allows to easily create a sing-along version of any song.

Melody Mania's song editor combines features that previously required multiple tools.
Plus, you can quickly switch between editing and testing your song.

  • Add notes via button tapping or by dictating lyrics
  • Edit notes via drag & drop
  • Change lyrics
  • Add videos and images
  • Edit song properties
  • Copy & paste notes
  • Import MIDI files
  • Assign notes to different players
  • Quality analysis and hints
  • Isolate vocals of songs for pitch detection and speech recognition
  • …and much more!

With all these features, creating a sing-along version for a game has never been easier.

 Customizable - Made with Unity 

Why an open song format?

Imagine that you would buy another singing game for your current console, only to find that the vendor shuts down the online features, or you lose your song collection when changing the device.

This won't happen with Melody Mania because it can evolve with the future as long as it has a contributing community behind it.

Made with Unity

Melody Mania is built on a professional game engine and modern tools to provide the best experience for developers and players.

Beyond Melody Mania

Melody Mania has its roots in the open source project UltraStar Play and is still contributing to this ecosystem. By supporting Melody Mania, you support its hard working developers to bring more sing-along fun to live.
